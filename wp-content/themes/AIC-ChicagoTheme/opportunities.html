Volunteer

The programs and services offered at the AIC would not happen without the many volunteers who contribute their time, energy and thoughtfulness! Thank you. Currently, our Community Coordinator organizes our volunteers and keeps them up to date on volunteer opportunities.

Our primary needs at this time are very basic:
  • Volunteer Coordinator - schedule, document and manage all volunteer activities.
  • Reception - any block of hours between 9am-5pm Monday through Friday.
  • Janitorial - help to sweep, mop and clean all areas of the Center. We can use people everyday.
